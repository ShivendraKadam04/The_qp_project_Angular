THE QUR'AN PROJECT

The Qur'an Project primary aim it to produce high quality products serving the study of the Qur'an and facilitate free distribution of them world-wide.

Our flagship product, "The Qur'an Project: English translation of the Qur'an with Surah introductions and appendices" has been distributed to over 80 countries worldwide and is widely seen as the "go to copy" for Da'wah. There have been major advertising campaigns in the UK, (i.e. TV, billboards, newspapers, radio) offering the free copy of Qur'an to non-Muslims across the world.

We distribute copies to Masjids, Schools, Prisons and Da'wah Stalls.

The Qur'an Project team comprises of an all-volunteer team based in the UK and we follow the Qur'an and Sunnah.
